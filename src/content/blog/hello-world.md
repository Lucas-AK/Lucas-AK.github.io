Hi and welcome. I've decided to start up this blog.

Mostly to have a place to put  notes that don't fit in one of my 7 different note-taking apps.
Writing things down forces me to actually understand them, and also in a manner where others can understand them.

> "If it is possible to cut a word out, always cut it out."
>
> <cite>- George Orwell</cite>

I would also like to get better at writing in English, since my grammar is probably terrible.

## There are many blogs, but this one is mine.

I've laid down some rules for myself for what I write here.
1) A post must include a picture of something.
2) Any kind of AI assistance is forbidden. I think we can all agree that there is enough slop text around these days.
3) It should be related to something I am currenly working on, either at work or at home.

I'll go back and review these rules at some point. They are meant to be broken after all.
